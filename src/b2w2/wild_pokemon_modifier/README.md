# Wild Pokemon Modifier

## Usage

 * Press Select to receive 649 Master Balls and 100 Ultra Balls.
 * Discard Master Balls to meet the Pokémon Dex number and discard 
   Ultra Balls to meet the Pokemon level. 
 * Press R for the encounter.
